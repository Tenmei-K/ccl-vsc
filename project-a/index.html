What we know so far about transparent swimmer (tentative)?
Transparent swimmers send out signals of different colors and in different frequencies, and we observe them to distinguish their different states. They react differently to food, creatures of their kind, and things that seem like their species. They will undergo amitosis after certain times of feeding.
